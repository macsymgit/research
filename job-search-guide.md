## 8. Recommended Search Strategy

### Architecture (BAS + MArch) Graduates

1. **During co-op**: WaterlooWorks is your primary source
2. **Professional boards**: OAA and RAIC job boards for Canadian licensure-track roles
3. **Industry boards**: Archinect, Dezeen Jobs, AIA Career Center — the big three for North America
4. **Networking**: LinkedIn is essential for firm connections and alumni outreach
5. **Market data**: Canada Job Bank for labour market reports
6. **Recruiters**: Register with DAVRON (US) and DMC/Hays (Canada) — free for candidates
7. **Urban design interest?** Add Planetizen and APA to your search
8. **Alternative careers?** Out of Architecture for tech, real estate, and consulting roles

### Architectural Engineering (BASc) Graduates

1. **During co-op**: WaterlooWorks is primary; supplement with LinkedIn and firm career pages
2. **Canadian P.Eng. roles**: Engineering Careers (engineeringcareers.ca), Canada Job Bank, ACEC career center
3. **US PE roles**: ASHRAE jobs, ASCE careers, NSPE career center, USAJOBS
4. **Direct applications**: Target WSP, Stantec, Arup, RJC, Entuitive career pages
5. **Green building/sustainability**: CaGBC, USGBC career center, Passive House Canada, ECO Canada
6. **Building envelope/science**: LinkedIn (300+ jobs), Indeed, and directly target RJC, Entuitive, Stantec
7. **Recruiters**: Register with AXIS, Hays, PHM Search for passive job searching

### 3D Animation (Sheridan) Graduates

1. **Sheridan Industry Day** is your single highest-value event — employers fly in to recruit
2. **Portfolio platform**: ArtStation is essential; studios actively scout portfolios there
3. **Niche boards first**: AWN, ArtStation Jobs, Cartoon Brew, Hitmarker — higher signal-to-noise
4. **Studio career pages directly**: Many positions appear there before hitting job boards
5. **Networking**: LinkedIn for connecting with recruiters and studio professionals
6. **Canadian markets**: Vancouver (VFX hub), Toronto (IP/children's content), Montreal (tax credits)
7. **Freelance building**: Contra (no commission), ArtStation Hire, Upwork for side projects
8. **Recruiters**: Rachelle Lewis Talent and I Spy Recruiting for studio placements
