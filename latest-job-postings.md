## 7. Key Observations

### Architecture
- **Salary transparency** is highest on Archinect and Canada Job Bank; Dezeen and AIA rarely list compensation
- **Archinect** skews toward US private-practice roles ($65K–$200K visible range)
- **AIA Career Center** offers a good mix of firm roles and institutional/public-sector positions
- **Canada Job Bank** shows ~12 architect (NOC 21200) postings nationally — smaller market but high quality

### Architectural Engineering
- **Structural engineers are in strong demand** across Canada, with postings from coast to coast
- **US structural roles on ASCE** show higher salary ceilings, with remote positions offering up to $200K
- **WSP's Centre Block (Parliament) rehabilitation** in Ottawa is a landmark project currently hiring
- **Green building roles** are emerging — the City of Vancouver's "Building Policy Engineer" at $114K is a unique example
- **Key skills in demand**: P.Eng./PE, Revit, BIM360, ASHRAE standards, OBC/NBC codes

### 3D Animation / VFX
- **Vancouver and Montreal are the hottest markets** for animation/VFX hiring in Canada
- **Sony Imageworks (50+ openings)** and **DNEG** have the most open positions right now
- **ILM has 40+ roles** mostly based in Vancouver
- **Pixar has very few openings** (only 3, all technical/research — no artist roles)
- **Gaming industry pays top dollar** for senior VFX — NVIDIA reaches $287K, EA and Blizzard $150K+
- **Cartoon Brew** no longer operates a standalone job board (moved to social channels)
- **ICAD internship at Sony Imageworks** — applications closing March 31, 2026
