## Why This Works — Quick Rationale

**1. Required-course coverage is complete.**
The pair (BIO 1A03 + 1M03), (CHEM 1A03 + 1AA3), and (MATH 1LS3 + 1LT3) is the standard Level II prerequisite combination across virtually every Honours Life Sciences specialization at McMaster.

**2. GPA risk is balanced across both terms.**
Both terms now have 3 hard cores + 2 lighter electives. You avoid stacking all the GPA-risky bio/chem/math in one term.

**3. Premed-strategic electives.**
PSYCH 1X03 and KIN 1YY3 both feed directly into MCAT content (P/S and B/B sections). You're getting GPA boost AND useful background, not just empty credit.

**4. One McMaster signature course kept.**
SUSTAIN 1S03 is a small-class inquiry course — valuable for the soft-skill profile that some Level II programs and BHSc Level-2 transfer reviewers care about.

## Year 1 Action Checklist (in order)

### Before enrollment opens
- [ ] **Confirm Level II target programs.** Open `mapsci.ca` and note the specific Year 1 prerequisites for your top 2–3 Level II choices (e.g., Honours Life Sci flagship, Origins of Disease, Sensory Motor Systems, Biology & Pharmacology Co-op).
- [ ] **Verify the MATH pair.** Most streams accept MATH 1LS3 + 1LT3; some (e.g., Biomedical Discovery & Commercialization) prefer MATH 1A03 + 1AA3 (more rigorous). If you're aiming at one of those, switch the math pair.
- [ ] **Check Physics requirement.** If you do *not* have SPH4U from Grade 12, plan **PHYSICS 1L03** for Year 2. If you have SPH4U, you can take **PHYSICS 1A03** in Year 2. Almost no Life Sci stream requires Physics in Year 1.
- [ ] **Read current Simple Syllabus pages** for every elective on this list — instructor and grading scheme change yearly.
- [ ] **Sanity-check on Rate My Courses (`ratemycourses.io/mcmaster`)** that KIN 1YY3 is still well-regarded for current instructor.

### When enrollment opens
- [ ] Enroll in core BIO + CHEM + MATH first (these fill fast).
- [ ] Enroll in PSYCH 1X03 next (also popular — fills quickly).
- [ ] Enroll in SUSTAIN 1S03 (smaller class, fills fast).
- [ ] Enroll in KIN 1YY3 last (usually has capacity).

### During the year
- [ ] **Y1 GPA target: 3.85+ (11.0+/12.0)**. This is the threshold that keeps every Level II option open, including BHSc Level-2 transfer.
- [ ] Hit office hours for CHEM 1A03 and MATH 1LS3 in the first 4 weeks — these are the Year 1 weed-out courses.
- [ ] Track your term GPA every term, not just year-end. If anything dips below 3.7, course-correct immediately.
- [ ] No heavy ECs in Year 1 (per `mcmaster-life-sciences.md:688`). One club + light volunteering is plenty.

## Alternative Substitutions (If Something Above Doesn't Work)

| If you can't get... | Substitute with... |
|---|---|
| KIN 1YY3 | **ECON 1B03** (Micro) — different but similarly GPA-friendly; counts as Social Science breadth |
| PSYCH 1X03 | **ANTHROP 1AA3** (Sex, Food, and Death) — humanities-side, popular |
| SUSTAIN 1S03 | **INDIGST 1A03 / 1AA3** — also small-class, well-regarded, and knocks out the Indigenous Studies graduation requirement |
| SCIENCE 1A03 (if swapping for GPA safety) | **ASTRON 1F03** (Introductory Astronomy) — classic bird course; Faculty-of-Science breadth |

**For BHSc Level-2 transfer hopefuls only:** Replace one of the GPA-boost electives with a writing-heavy humanities course like **PHILOS 1B03** (Bio-Medical Ethics) or **ENGLISH 1A03**. Reviewers value visible writing rigor over a transcript of pure bird courses.

## What NOT to Worry About in Year 1

- **Physics** — not required Year 1 for Life Sci Gateway; can wait until Year 2.
- **Electives that "look impressive"** — admissions to Level II is GPA-driven; transcript optics matter at the margin only.
- **Co-op decisions** — Life Sci Co-op application is in Year 2/3, not Year 1.
- **Major-specific courses (Origins of Disease, etc.)** — these are Level II declarations; just keep the prereqs open via the schedule above.
